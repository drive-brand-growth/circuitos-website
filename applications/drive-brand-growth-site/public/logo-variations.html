RECOMMENDATIONS

For embroidery/merch: ALL CAPS works better - cleaner stitching, more readable at small sizes

For digital/web: Mixed case (CircuitOS) feels more modern/tech startup - like macOS, iOS, etc.

™ placement: Should go after the full mark "CircuitOS™" or "CIRCUITOS™" - that's the standard
